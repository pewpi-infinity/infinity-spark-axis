# ⭐ Trend Intelligence — Why Ideas & Sites Fly

Observed drivers:
- **Timing:** alignment with news cycles and policy moments
- **Clarity:** simple explanations of complex mechanisms
- **Structure:** scannable sections, summaries, tables
- **Cadence:** consistent updates build trust
- **Internal Linking:** keeps readers moving
- **Visual Hierarchy:** charts > prose for comparisons

Trends are **descriptive**, not prescriptive.
